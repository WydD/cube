Loïc Petit's cube kode

You'll find here, pieces of program/kode I made for Rubik's cube and other puzzles. This is mainly a sandbox for me, but you can find some useful stuff.

  • An adaptation of imagecube-scrambler for NxN
  • A BTC generator for square-1
  • A re-adaptation of the official SQ1 scrambler to use svg/vml via raphaël instead of wz_jsgraphics
  • Simple WCA-Approved scrambler (one single file) (2010 regulations, 2009 also available)
  • A BTC optimal solver (complement of Jaap's diagram)
  • 2x2 first face study (in french, dedicated english page coming soon)
